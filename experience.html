Experience

University of Nebraska-Lincoln

Graduate Research Assistant (2023 – Present)
Graduate Teaching Assistant (2024 – Present)

As a teaching assistant, I supported CIVE 202 – Civil Engineering Analysis II, a project-based programming course, and CIVE 361 – Principles of Transportation Engineering, covering highway design, traffic operations, and planning.

TrafQuest

Senior Transportation Engineer (2019 – 2023)
Transportation Engineer (2013 – 2019)

At TrafQuest, I led over 100 projects across the UAE and MENA region, including transportation master plans, traffic impact studies, and multimodal integration initiatives. My work involved forecasting demand, analyzing traffic operations, evaluating multimodal connectivity, simulating conditions with advanced tools, and preparing technical reports. I managed projects spanning master plans, traffic impact studies, freight transport, parking, and evacuation planning. I was project manager for most of these efforts, ensuring sustainability and feasibility in all delivered solutions.
