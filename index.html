About

I am a PhD student in Civil Engineering at the University of Nebraska–Lincoln. My research focuses on AV safety, pedestrian–vehicle interactions, and machine learning in crash modeling. I aim to integrate advanced data sources and innovative methods to improve predictive accuracy and transportation equity.

Research

I study AV–pedestrian interactions, platoon stability, and consensus-aware AV behavior. I analyze safety indicators like PET and TTC using TGSIM datasets, and explore the role of StreetLight traffic volumes and Mapillary detections in crash analysis. My work compares GLMs and ML models in safety prediction.
